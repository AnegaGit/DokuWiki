====== Mental Map ======
The ability defines how good a character can memorize a map.
The mini map is controlled by the ability.

**Lost in a maze?**
==== Not Available ====
//What you see is what you get.//\\
You don't have a feeling for the area around yourself.
==== Poor ====
//Do not stalk from behind!//\\
You feeling for the area around yourself is limited for the area in front of your eyes.
==== Good ====
//All around clear.//\\
Your feeling for the area around yourself has a limited range.
==== Excellent ====
//Bird eyes view!//\\
Your feeling for the area around yourself can be extended to a larger area.
