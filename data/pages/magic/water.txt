====== Water Magic ======
//Everything flows more or less//\\
It is one with everything that changes. Some call this school of magic the magic of life.\\
Here you will find healing spells.\\
Shields or attacks made of water or ice are rather weak.

Color: Blue\\
Gem  : Sapphire
