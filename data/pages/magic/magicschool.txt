====== Magic Schools ======
{{:magic:magiccircle.jpg?400|Magic Schools}}\\
There are 4 different magic schools known:\\
  * [[magic:fire|Fire]]
  * [[magic:water|Water]]
  * [[magic:earth|Earth]]
  * [[magic:wind|Wind / Air]]

Each represents a different set of magic capabilities. Nobody is able to become a master in all schools.
