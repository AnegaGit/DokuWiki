====== Threeleaf ======
Threeleaf can often be found in large groups in the broadleaf forest and swamps.

{{vegetation:threeleaf.jpg|Threeleaf}}
All 4 phases and 3 findings.
===== Live Cycle =====
The herb has 4 phases with different findings.
^Phase^Finding^when collected^Average duration^when timeout^
|young|up to 3 leafes|dies|5 days|bloomy|
|bloomy|-||4 days|ripe|
|ripe|up to 4 berries|may get destroyed|2 days|old|
|old|up to 1 root|destroyed|4 days|dies|
