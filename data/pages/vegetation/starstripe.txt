====== Starstripe ======
Starstripe grows in groups on meadows and in light forests.

{{ vegetation:starstripe.jpg |Starstripe}}
All 5 phases and 2 findings.
===== Live Cycle =====
The herb has 5 phases with different findings. It cycles about 5 times through the adult phase.
^Phase^Finding^when collected^Average duration^when timeout^
|young| ||7 days|adult|
|adult| ||2 days|bloomy|
|bloomy| ||4 days|ripe|
|ripe|about 6 starstripe berries|90% adult\\ 10% dies|4 days|80% adult\\ 20% old|
|old|about 2 starstripe leafs|dies|7 days|dies|
