====== Health Diagnosis ======
The ability defines how exact a character can dedicate the health state of other persons.

**The next please!**
==== Not Available ====
//He is still alive!//\\
You can just tell if someone else is alive.
==== Poor ====
//So much blood, where does it all come from?//\\
You can not recognize more than the obvious health state.
These states are:
  * unharmed
  * slightly wounded
  * wounded
  * badly wounded
  * near death
==== Good ====
//Be careful when they take the knife!//\\
You can estimate the health status of each person examined relatively well.
==== Excellent ====
//Doctors are great as long as you don't need them.//\\
You can see the state of health of each person being examined accurately.
