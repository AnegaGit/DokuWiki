====== Dark Vision======
The ability defines how good a character can see without light.
The amount of ambient light is controlled by the ability.\\
**Everything went black**
==== Not Available ====
//Is there a hand in front of your face?//\\
Without a strong light source you are lost in the night or in a dungeon. Everything might become black at night.
==== Poor ====
//The stars are sometimes enough.//\\
A little light light the stars show you some of your environment. But in a dungeon everything might become black.
==== Good ====
//Cat Eye//\\
As long as there is any light source you can can clearly recognize the environment. But in a dungeon it may not be that easy.
==== Excellent ====
//Torches are overrated.//\\
You don't need any light source, even in the most dark dungeon.
