====== Read and Write ======
The ability defines whether a character can read and write.

**Your crosses here, please!**
==== Not Available ====
//Learning by heart is the nuts and bolts. I'm really good at it.//\\
Letters are a mystery. You are illiterate and can not even read signposts or labels.
==== Poor ====
//E... En... Entrance!//\\
You can read labels and signposts but neither parchments nor books. You cannot write.
==== Good ====
//I don't like blue fingers.//\\
You can read everything and write labels and signposts.
==== Excellent ====
//Those with education//\\
You can read and write everything.
