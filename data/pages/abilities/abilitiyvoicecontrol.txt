====== Voice control ======
The ability defines the distance your character can be heard.

**Say that again, please!**
==== Not Available ====
//Coughs ...//\\
You are barely understandable. Most will think you are dumb.
==== Poor ====
//Louder!//\\
You shouldn't try to speak to more people. And you better run instead call for help.
==== Good ====
//I understand.//\\
You don't have any issues with communication.
==== Excellent ====
//Be quite!//\\
Your voice carries far.
