====== Compass ======
The ability defines how good a character can identify the north direction.
The compass is controlled by the ability.

**Shall we turn to left or right?**
==== Not Available ====
//The town is 5 minutes away and I'm lost!//\\
You have no idea where north might be.
==== Poor ====
//That's where we comming from ... but.//\\
Your compass knows 4 directions.
==== Good ====
//That way looks better.//\\
Your compass knows 8 directions..
==== Excellent ====
//Follow me, I know the way!//\\
Your compass shows exact north.
