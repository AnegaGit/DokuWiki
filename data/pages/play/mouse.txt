====== Mouse Control ======
See also [[play:key|control keys]].
==== Movement ====
^Key^Action^Effect^
|left|click on item or person|select|
|left|click on not selectable position|Move to position|
|left|hold|Follow mouse|
|right|click on item or person|[[contextmenu|Open the context menu]]|
|middle wheel|hold|camera angle and altitude|
|middle wheel|scroll|camera distance|
|middle wheel|click|toggle camera behind player or not|
==== Drag&Drop ====
