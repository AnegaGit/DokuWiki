====== Playtime ======
Some functions depends on the time, a character is actively played.
  * Experience, distributable for skills
  * Attribute points

A character collects playtime by actively participating in the game.\\
Standing idle and doing nothing is considered inactive does not get any game time.

Different activities extend the time to the next necessary activity (sorted descending by influence)
  - Talking or perform an emotion
  - Moving or turning
