====== Lightgallery ======
How to set up Lightgallery for the webpage.

''{lightgallery galleryName=development type=local path=images/development/2019-03-03_Chat previewWidth=150}Have a look to the minimap{/lightgallery}''

|galleryName|all pictures in a gallery are shown together in the show|
|path|path or file shown in preview\\ must be directory or full file name|
