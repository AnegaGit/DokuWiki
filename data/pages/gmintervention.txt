====== Divine Interventions ======
Core game manager are entitled to make changes to the settings of characters.//
The changes do not require the consent of the player. Adjustments can be communicated to the player or activated without knowledge of the person concerned.//
Adjustments must be documented in the GM Tool.
===== Divine Punishment =====
Violations of the Laws of the Game are preferable to answer with temporary or permanent restrictions on the attributes and abilities of the affected character.
==== Examples ====
The character loses abilities. It is e.g. no longer possible to adapt the displayed name or to build or change public buildings.

The total skill cap of the character is reduced.

The character continuously loses attributes. If no attributes can be reduced, the character dies (will be deleted) and can no longer be played.
