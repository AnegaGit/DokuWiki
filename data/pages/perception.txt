====== Perception ======
How much a character can see depends on the attribute perception.
^Attribute^Effect^Min^Max^Used nonlinear curve^
|Perception|range of visible names|15|60|8|
|Perception|zoom distance|57|2|12|
=== Visible Names ===
A character can see the name and health state of other character, NPC and monster within a certain distance.
=== Zoom Distance ===
The camera angle can be reduced in first person view. This allows to examine details such as monster equipment or road signs from a distance. The values are in a range of a row focal length of 20 - 350.
